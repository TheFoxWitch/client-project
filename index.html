At lefty’s guitars, we strive to offer the best quality guitars along with great customer service. We are based in Austin, Texas - home of Austin City Limits and South By Southwest. Because of the thriving music scene, Austin has been called the “Music Capital of the World”! This diverse music scene is part of the DNA of Lefty’s. We strive to meet the needs of musicians from different styles of music. Lefty’s Guitars was established in 1999. Yes, that’s right, we have been here at the same location for 18 years. The owner/janitor Gregg Andrews attributes the success of the store to the relationships built with the diverse clientele. There are many things unique about South Austin Music. One of our biggest assets are the people that work here.
